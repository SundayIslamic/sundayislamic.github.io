Our Vision

We are dedicated to propagating the Quran and Islamic principles, as emphasized by Prophet Mohammed (peace be upon him) in his farewell sermon, famously known as Hujjatul-Wida, delivered to a large number of companions.

He asked his companions whether he had conveyed Allah's Deen, and they unanimously said yes. Entrusting them with the responsibility to share this message with both those present and those not, inspired by this legacy, we strive to spread the teachings of Islam and the Quran, fulfilling the Prophet's vbrew install ghision of spreading divine guidance to every corner of the world.

About Us

Welcome to the Sunday Islamic School India, a nurturing online platform created to the spiritual and academic development of young minds in India. Founded and run by Brother Khalid, Moualana & Hafiz e Quran and a graduate of the esteemed Darul Uloom Nadwatul Ulama in Lucknow, our goal is to strives and instill Islamic values, teachings, and knowledge in students of all ages. With a focus on Quranic studies, Islamic history, and moral education, we aim to cultivate a deep love for Islam while encouraging students to apply these values in their everyday lives.

Our dedicated teachers work to create an engaging and inclusive learning experience, where students can explore their faith, develop their character, and strengthen their connection with Allah (SWT). Whether it's learning to recite the Quran, understanding the life of the Prophet (PBUH), or practicing good manners, we provide a well-rounded curriculum tailored to each student's level.

We believe that every child deserves to have a strong foundation in Islam, and we are committed to helping them grow into responsible, confident, and knowledgeable members of the Indian Muslim community.

Know your Teacher

At our Sunday Islamic School India, we are blessed with dedicated and knowledgeable teachers who are passionate about fostering the growth of our students in both faith and character. Each teacher brings a unique blend of Islamic scholarship, practical life experience, and a commitment to nurturing young minds. Our teachers strive to create a warm, inclusive environment where students not only learn about Islamic teachings but also build a strong moral foundation to guide them in everyday life. Their goal is to ensure that each student gains a deeper understanding of Islam, develops a personal connection with their faith, and grows into a responsible, caring member of the community. Whether it’s through engaging lessons on the Quran, interactive discussions on Islamic history, or practical life skills, our teachers are here to support and guide every student on their journey.
